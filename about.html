About Us

We are a group of students from the Information School's Technical Foundations class. Comprising of mostly Informatics prospectives, we hope to use the skills we learned this quarter to build an interactive data visualization platform to share insights and spread further awareness on the global carbon cycle using data gathered within the last 50 years.
